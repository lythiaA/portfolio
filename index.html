Technical Skills E-Learning Course

Comprehensive Guide to MySQL Joins & Aggregate Functions

The Challenge

Instructional Problem

Learners new to SQL often find concepts like JOINs and Aggregate Functions to be abstract and difficult to apply. They may understand the syntax but struggle to combine these functions to solve complex, real-world data problems. This gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application can hinder their confidence and effectiveness.

The Learner Persona

The target audience includes aspiring data analysts, junior developers, and students with a basic understanding of SQL. They need a well-structured, interactive learning experience that bridges the gap from simple queries to advanced data manipulation and analysis, complete with opportunities for practice and assessment.

The Solution & My Process

I developed a comprehensive e-learning course using the Articulate 360 suite. The course was built primarily in Articulate Rise 360 to create a clean, modern, and fully responsive learning experience. Custom interactions and knowledge checks were built in Articulate Storyline 360 and embedded into the Rise course to enhance engagement. The curriculum is scaffolded to guide learners logically from foundational concepts to practical coding applications.

Key Features & Skills Demonstrated

Articulate 360 Suite

Utilized Articulate Rise for rapid development and a responsive design, and integrated custom-built Articulate Storyline blocks for more complex interactions and quizzes.

Scaffolded Curriculum

Designed a logical learning path that starts with a scenario and introduction, covers foundational concepts individually (Aggregates, Joins), and then combines them in practical coding exercises.

Knowledge Checks & Quizzes

Incorporated both a "Joins Knowledge Check" for formative assessment and a final "Coding Quiz" as a summative evaluation to measure learner comprehension and application skills.

Clean & Intuitive UI

Leveraged Articulate Rise to create a clean, modern, and easy-to-navigate user interface that is fully responsive for learning on any device.

Contextual Learning

The course begins with a "Quick Overview and Scenario Introduction" to immediately provide context and relevance, helping learners understand the 'why' behind the technical skills.

Completion & Certification

A completion certificate is included as the final step, providing learners with a sense of accomplishment and a tangible outcome for their efforts.

Measuring Success

Proposed Evaluation Plan

Learner Satisfaction:

"Do learners find the course to be engaging, well-structured, and relevant to their career goals?"

Measured via: A post-course survey to gather feedback on course structure, clarity of explanations, and overall satisfaction.

Skill Acquisition:

"Can learners demonstrate proficiency in writing MySQL queries using Joins and Aggregate Functions after completing the course?"

Measured via: Analyzing learner performance on the "Joins Knowledge Check" and the final "Coding Quiz," tracking pass rates and identifying common error areas.
